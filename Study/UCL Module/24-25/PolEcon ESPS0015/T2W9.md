# Economic Voting

- Incumbent party vote share of two-party voting  *postive related linearly* with weighted-average annualised per capita of income

## Moral Hazard  ( Principal Agent Problem)

| Principal   | Agent       | Hidden Action                    |
| ----------- | ----------- | -------------------------------- |
| Government  | Institution | Quality of the product           |
| Interviewer | Interviewee | Truth of the questionnaire       |
| Voter       | Politician  | Economic \ Politics \ corruption |
**Key**
1. Expensive monitor cost
2. Incomplete Contract
3. Asymmetric Information
4. Conflict of Interests

**Solution**
1. Performance measure
2. Align incentives
3. penalty clauses
4. Complete Contracts
5. Contingents Renewal
