# Veto Player Theory 

- decision making process
- *Veto Player* : political player who can make policy/legislation change.

# Theory

## Simple setup:

1. Image player A, B, C, are scattered plot on the page, with their indifference interest circle centring themselves 
2. **Status quo** : Positioning point where 3 circles intersect, every one is satisfied at this point, status quo
3. **The Win-set**: Where three interest circles conjuncts and the are overlapping, any policy lie in this area satisfies all 3's interest and hence is agreed. 
4. **The Core**: Connecting the 3 politicians (centre of the circle), the triangle is the core.

## Model Specification
### Independent Variables:

**Number of Veto Players**:
- number of interplayers matter per se
- looking for specific cases
**Ideological Distance**
- The greater the distance, the stable 
- Intuitively, more people, harder to reach agreement.
**Alternation**
- the more people, the stable
**Agenda Setting**
- The one set the agenda maximise their interest within winning set.
