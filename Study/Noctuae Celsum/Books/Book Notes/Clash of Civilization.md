# Introduction
## I： For the first time in history, global politics is both multipolar and multicivilizational 背景介绍：多极化&文明多元化：（冷战后）新世界格局首次到来

### 现代化不同于西化
Modernization is distinct from Westernization and is producing neither a universal civilization in any meaningful sense nor the Westernization of non-Western societies.
    
### Pre-Cold War： Multipolar within Western civilizations
    
[Go to annotation](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/G5WARJHL?page=3&annotation=undefined) “During most of human existence, contacts between civilizations were intermittent or nonexistent.” ([Huntington, p. 3](zotero://select/library/items/Z7V88AN3))
    
### Since the Modern Era -->  Pre-Cold War
    
[Go to annotation](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/G5WARJHL?page=3&annotation=undefined) “the nation states of the West, Britain, France, Spain, Austria, Prussia, Germany, the United States, and others……also **expanded, conquered, colonized, or decisively influenced every other civilization**” ([Huntington, p. 3](zotero://select/library/items/Z7V88AN3))
    
### During the Cold War： Bipolar with 3 parts
    
Mostly wealthy and **democratic** societies, led by the **_United States_**
       | --> engaged in a pervasive, ideological, political, economic, and military competition
    A group of (somewhat) poorer **communist** societies, led by the **_Soviet Union_**
    
Conflict occurred in **Third World countries** ：Poor， _lacked political stability, were recently independent, claiming to be non-aligned_
    
### Post-Cold War: Shifts to Cultural Identity Dependence
    
- People define themselves in terms of _ancestry, religion, language, history, values, customs, and institutions_.
- They identify with _cultural groups_: tribes, ethnic groups, religious communities, nations, and, at the broadest level, civilizations.
- People use politics not just to advance their interests but **_also to define their identity._**

## II： The Balance of power among civilizations is shifting. 客观局势：文明多元化矛盾初显

- 总体局势：西消东长 The West is declining in relative influence; Asian civilizations are expanding their economic, military, and. political strength
- 格外关注：伊斯兰 Islam is exploding demographically with destabilizing consequences for Muslim countries and their neighbor.
- 非西方国家 Non-Western civilizations generally reaffirm the value of their own cultures.

## III： A civilization-based world order is emerging. 理论建构：基于文明认同的合纵连横

- 物以类聚，国以文明“群分” Societies sharing cultural affinities cooperate with each other
- 文明转化失败——普世文明主义的败北. Efforts to shift societies from one civilization to another are unsuccessful
- 文明的领头羊效应——仅限于穆斯林？Countries group themselves around the lead or core states of their civilization.

## IV：Conflict Between Western Universalism and diverse Civilisations 举例论证：世界格局的现在进行时

- 西方普世文化入侵 The West's universalist pretensions increasingly bring it into conflict with other civilizations, most seriously with Islam and China
- 穆斯林“亲缘国家联盟”引发危机 At the local level fault line wars, largely between Muslims and non-Muslims, generate "kin-country rallying," the threat of broader escalation, and hence efforts by core states to halt these wars.

## V： The survival of the West & Avoidance of a global war 西方普世性危机与文明战争——直面现实，拥抱多元

- 西方：美国，文明联盟与普适性的消解 The survival of the West depends on Americans reaffirming their Western identity and Westerners accepting their civilization as unique not universal； And uniting to renew and preserve it against challenges from non-Western societies
- 承认多元化 Avoidance of a global war of civilizations depends on world leaders accepting and cooperating to maintain the multicivilizational character of global politics.
