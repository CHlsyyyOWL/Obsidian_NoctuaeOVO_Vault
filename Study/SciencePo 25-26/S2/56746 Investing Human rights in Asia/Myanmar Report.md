**— Speaker Notes (English) —**

The Karen National Liberation Army, or KNLA, is the armed wing of the Karen National Union — the KNU — Myanmar's oldest ethnic armed organisation, founded in 1947 with armed conflict beginning in 1949. The Karen people number around seven million, concentrated in Karen State, the Bago Region, and along the Thai-Myanmar border, with significant diaspora communities in Thailand, Australia, and the United States. The KNU's original goal was full independence — the creation of a Karen homeland called Kawthoolei — but this has since shifted to seeking federalism and genuine autonomy within a democratic Myanmar. The KNLA is led by Commander-in-Chief General Saw Johnny, operating out of a mobile headquarters near the Thai border.

_(Turn to current status section)_

As of 2021, the KNLA fields approximately fifteen thousand troops across seven brigades plus a dedicated Special Forces unit, administering territory inhabited by around eight hundred thousand people and providing education and healthcare in areas it governs. Funding comes from border trade taxation, timber and gem revenues along the Thai-Myanmar corridor, and diaspora donations. It is important to state clearly that credible sources do not attribute KNLA financing to narcotics — a claim more accurately associated with the Karen National Army, the KNA, which is an entirely separate organisation. Politically, the KNLA refuses any ceasefire that excludes the NUG and insists Karen political rights must be structurally addressed. The Junta responded to the KNLA's growing influence by formally designating the KNU and KNLA as terrorist organisations in August 2025 — widely interpreted as an attempt to delegitimise the resistance rather than a meaningful legal determination. On recent operations, notable actions include the brief seizure of positions around Myawaddy in April 2024, the recapture of former headquarters Manerplaw in December 2024, and a scam compound raid detaining two thousand foreign nationals in November 2025. The KNLA is increasingly recognised by the international community as a legitimate stakeholder in Myanmar's political future.

_(Turn to allegations section)_

This brings us to two sensitive allegations that require careful handling. First, on human trafficking and KK Park — the KNLA is routinely but incorrectly linked to these criminal operations. KK Park and the Shwe Kokko scam hubs are controlled by the Karen National Army, the KNA, a separate Junta-aligned organisation descended from the 1994 DKBA split, which was sanctioned by US Treasury OFAC in May 2025 for running criminal enterprises on an industrial scale. The KNLA is a distinct organisation — and in fact, it was the KNLA that raided a scam compound in November 2025 and handed detainees to Thai authorities, placing it in direct opposition to what the KNA profits from. This conflation of the two organisations is a recurring problem in media coverage and worth correcting explicitly. Second, on child soldiers — the 2010 UN listing is real and should not be dismissed. However, the same report records in paragraph 88 that Myanmar government obstruction prevented the UN from formalising an action plan with the KNLA, despite the KNU having actively invited monitoring. This is a meaningful distinction between allegation and verified conduct that the KNU itself placed on record.

**— 演讲稿（中文版）—**

克伦民族解放军，简称KNLA，是克伦民族联盟（KNU）的武装力量。KNU是缅甸历史最悠久的少数民族武装政治组织，成立于1947年，武装冲突正式于1949年爆发。克伦族人口约700万，主要集中在克伦邦、勃固省及泰缅边境一带，在泰国、澳大利亚和美国均有规模可观的海外侨民社区。KNU最初的目标是建立完全独立的克伦人家园"格都丽"，但现已调整为在民主联邦框架内寻求真正的自治权。KNLA现任总司令为General Saw Johnny，指挥部设于泰缅边境附近的机动位置。

**（翻到当前状况部分）**

截至2021年，KNLA拥有约1.5万名士兵，编制为七个旅加一支专属特种部队，对约80万人口的居住区域实施实际行政管理，在控制区提供教育和医疗服务。资金来源主要是泰缅边境贸易税收、木材和宝石行业收入以及海外侨民捐款。需要明确指出：可靠来源不支持KNLA参与毒品贸易的说法，这一说法通常是将KNLA与克伦民族军（KNA）混淆所致——KNA是一个与KNLA截然不同的独立组织。在政治立场上，KNLA拒绝任何不纳入NUG的停火方案，并坚持克伦族政治权利必须得到实质性解决。军政府于2025年8月正式将KNU及KNLA列为恐怖组织，被国际社会普遍解读为企图以法律手段打压抵抗力量，而非真正意义上的法律认定。近期重要行动包括：2024年4月短暂夺取妙瓦底周边关键据点、2024年12月重新夺回前总部曼纳普劳，以及2025年11月突袭诈骗窝点拘押2000名外国公民。KNLA正日益被国际社会视为讨论缅甸政治未来时不可回避的合法利益相关方。

**（以上是KNLA的基本概况，但围绕这一组织还存在一些争议性指控，让我们翻到下一页来仔细看看。）**

**（翻到争议部分）**

这里涉及两个需要谨慎处理的敏感指控。首先是KK园区与人口贩卖的问题。KNLA经常被错误地与这些犯罪活动挂钩，但这是一个身份混淆的问题。KK园区和实皆科科诈骗特区由克伦民族军（KNA）控制——KNA是源于1994年DKBA分裂、与军政府有合作关系的独立组织，因大规模经营犯罪业务于2025年5月遭到美国财政部OFAC制裁。KNLA是完全独立的组织，且正是KNLA在2025年11月主动突袭诈骗窝点并将被拘者移交泰国当局，立场与KNA截然相反。这种对两个组织的混淆在媒体报道中反复出现，值得在报告中明确纠正。关于儿童兵问题——2010年的联合国列名是真实存在的，不应被轻描淡写。然而，同一份报告第88段也明确记录了缅甸政府阻止联合国接触非国家武装组织的事实，而KNU本身曾主动邀请监察。这是指控与经核实行为之间的重要区别，也是KNU自身留存于正式记录中的立场。
