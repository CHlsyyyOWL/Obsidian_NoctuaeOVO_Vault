## Methodology 101 
Preparatory Phase 
- by considering all the terms in an essay can lead to an the establishment of an plan or outline
	- gather all you know about the topic: an open regard on history and all its implication
	- Avoid venturing off the topic 
	- Determine what is relevant and is not relevant to the topic 

Organize your answer
- introduction: cntextualisation of the topic, presentation and analysis of the topic. apreview of the body of yoru essay
- Main body
	- decide on sections and subsections trastional sentences examples situated in their context to underpin your point 
		- each paragraph must be structure to address a spefici point 
		- your point must be supported by at least one example, either historical (event, historian figure) or historiographical (historian's opinion)
			- detailed needed: periodization for evens, name of an event
- conclusion: can be open-end
