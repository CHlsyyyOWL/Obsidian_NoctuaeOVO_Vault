# Materialist Conception of History

| Five Historical Periods    | Five Modes of production    | Five Ideologies           |
| -------------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Primitive communism<br>    | Hunter/Gather               | None                      |
| Ancient cities and empires | Slave agriculture           | Ancient Philosophy        |
| Medieval<br>               | Feudalism, trade and guilds | Christianity              |
| Modern period<br>          | Capitalism, Factories       | Enlightenment rationalism |
| Communism                  | Something entirely new      | Communism                 |
Historical Materialism:
- Argues that the mode of production drives ideological change
- It follows that if you are interested in critique your focus should on the material not the ideal

History is conceptualized as "class struggle"
- Each historical period transitions to the next through a revolution in the economic means of production
- leading to a change in the mode of production
