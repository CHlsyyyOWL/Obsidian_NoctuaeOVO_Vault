1. **Ray, A. (2025).**

_Interrogating Technological Leapfrogging in Asian Development and Growth Models: Toward a Critical Postcolonial Political Economy Approach._

_Social Media + Society._

🔗 [SAGE Link](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1177/20563051251341790) | [PDF](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/pdf/10.1177/20563051251341790)

该文研究 2008 危机后中国等亚洲国家如何通过国家主导的数字基础设施建设重组路径依赖，强调“国家—资本—技术复合体”重塑发展路径。

2. **Zhang, L. & Peck, J. (2023).**

_Statecraft, Path Plasticity and the Political Economy of Green Transitions in China._

_Environment and Planning A._

探讨绿色转型中，不同地方政府如何通过与中央政策“对话”，创造路径变异空间，强调“路径可塑性”。

3. **Wu, F. (2021).**

_Path dependence and the transformation of the state in urban China._

_Urban Studies, 58(4), 723–741._

[DOI](https://doi.org/10.1177/0042098020935752)

提出“制度重组并非自动响应市场”，而是危机与国家治理变迁交织的产物。城市中的土地财政和治理工具形成地方特有路径。

4. **Heilmann, S. & Shih, L. (2020).**

_Red Swan Reloaded: Crisis, Governance, and Institutional Change in China._

_Cambridge University Press._

以“治理性危机”为入口，解释中国如何在制度内实现“渐进性路径创造”。

5. **Huang, Y. & Chen, X. (2022).**

_Regional Institutional Varieties and Development Pathways in Post-reform China._

_Journal of Contemporary China, 31(134), 223–241._

比较不同区域制度配置与干部激励如何影响政策路径采纳。

6. **Chen, A. & Wang, J. (2019).**

_Institutional Lock-in and Path Creation: The Evolution of Local Government-Led Development Zones in China._

_Territory, Politics, Governance._

发展区制度的持续演化说明地方政府并非完全“被动执行者”，而是路径塑造者。

7. **Peck, J. & Zhang, J. (2022).**

_Crisis-driven innovation and the scalar politics of economic governance in China._

_Geoforum, 129, 10–22._

危机如何与尺度政治互动，推动某些地方出现“另类路径”。

8. **Lai, K. & Lin, G. C. S. (2020).**

_State-led financialization in China: Local government financing vehicles and the making of shadow banking._

_Economic Geography, 96(3), 211–232._

分析“财政金融复合体”如何形成地方路径依赖（如基建-地产模式）的制度锁定。

9. **Zhou, M. (2020).**

_Scaling the State: Policy Experimentation, Central-Local Relations, and the Transformation of Governance in China._

_The China Quarterly, 243, 731–753._

将“尺度政治”作为理解政策路径创新的钥匙，尤其是在地方主动性与中央试点授权的张力下。

10. **Xu, C. (2018).**

_Path dependency, institutional change and China’s reform trajectory._

_Structural Change and Economic Dynamics, 45, 47–56._
